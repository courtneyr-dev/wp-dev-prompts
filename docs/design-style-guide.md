# Design Style Guide for Vibe Coders

> **Don't know design terms? No problem.** Browse these styles, find one that feels right, and tell your AI assistant: "Use the [style name] aesthetic."

## Complete Style Library

### Minimalist
**The "less is more" approach**

```
Colors: White, off-white, one accent color
Typography: Clean sans-serif, lots of white space
Shapes: Simple, geometric
Feeling: Calm, focused, sophisticated
```

**Best for:** Productivity apps, portfolios, business sites
**Avoid if:** You need to convey warmth or playfulness

**Example prompt:**
> "Design a settings panel using Minimalist style - lots of white space, subtle gray borders, one blue accent for actions"

---

### Brutalist
**Raw, bold, unapologetic**

```
Colors: High contrast - black/white with one loud accent (red, yellow)
Typography: Heavy, sometimes ugly on purpose
Shapes: Blocky, no rounded corners
Feeling: Honest, bold, anti-corporate
```

**Best for:** Developer tools, art projects, statements
**Avoid if:** You need to seem approachable or friendly

**Example prompt:**
> "Design a file upload component in Brutalist style - thick black borders, no shadows, red error states, monospace text"

---

### Art Deco
**1920s glamour meets geometric precision**

```
Colors: Gold, black, deep navy, emerald, copper
Typography: Elegant serifs, all-caps headers with wide letter-spacing
Shapes: Geometric patterns, symmetry, decorative frames
Feeling: Luxurious, sophisticated, timeless
```

**Best for:** Premium products, hotels, fashion, finance
**Avoid if:** You need to seem casual or budget-friendly

**Example prompt:**
> "Design a pricing table in Art Deco style - gold borders on dark navy, geometric corner decorations, elegant serif headings"

---

### Art Nouveau
**Organic curves inspired by nature**

```
Colors: Muted greens, golds, purples, cream
Typography: Decorative headers, flowing letterforms
Shapes: Curved lines, floral motifs, asymmetric
Feeling: Artistic, romantic, natural elegance
```

**Best for:** Boutiques, spas, artisan products, galleries
**Avoid if:** You need to convey speed or efficiency

**Example prompt:**
> "Design a product card in Art Nouveau style - curved borders, subtle floral accents, warm cream background, flowing script for titles"

---

### Nordic / Scandinavian
**Light, functional, cozy**

```
Colors: White, light gray, pale blue, muted sage, warm wood tones
Typography: Clean sans-serif, comfortable reading sizes
Shapes: Simple, rounded corners, generous padding
Feeling: Calm, trustworthy, hygge (cozy)
```

**Best for:** Wellness, home goods, lifestyle, SaaS
**Avoid if:** You need high energy or excitement

**Example prompt:**
> "Design a dashboard widget in Nordic style - white card with subtle shadow, pale blue accents, rounded corners, plenty of breathing room"

---

### Cyberpunk
**Neon-lit dystopian future**

```
Colors: Neon pink, cyan, purple on black/dark gray
Typography: Tech fonts, glitch effects, terminal style
Shapes: Sharp angles, glowing borders, scanlines
Feeling: Futuristic, edgy, high-tech
```

**Best for:** Gaming, tech products, entertainment, crypto
**Avoid if:** You need to seem trustworthy or professional

**Example prompt:**
> "Design a stats display in Cyberpunk style - dark background, neon cyan borders that glow, hot pink accent for alerts, monospace numbers"

---

### Bauhaus
**Form follows function**

```
Colors: Primary colors (red, blue, yellow) + black, white
Typography: Geometric sans-serif, bold weights
Shapes: Circles, squares, triangles - pure geometry
Feeling: Modern, efficient, artistic but practical
```

**Best for:** Design tools, education, modern brands
**Avoid if:** You need warmth or organic feeling

**Example prompt:**
> "Design a toolbar in Bauhaus style - primary color icons on white, geometric shapes, bold sans-serif labels, clear visual hierarchy"

---

### Neo-Classical
**Traditional elegance, modern execution**

```
Colors: Cream, navy, burgundy, gold accents
Typography: Refined serifs, traditional proportions
Shapes: Symmetrical, columns, subtle ornamentation
Feeling: Established, trustworthy, refined
```

**Best for:** Law firms, universities, luxury real estate, finance
**Avoid if:** You need to seem innovative or disruptive

**Example prompt:**
> "Design a contact form in Neo-Classical style - cream background, navy text, subtle gold line accents, serif labels, symmetrical layout"

---

### Biophilic
**Nature-inspired, bringing outdoors in**

```
Colors: Greens, earth tones, sky blue, warm browns
Typography: Organic, slightly rounded sans-serifs
Shapes: Leaf-inspired curves, natural textures
Feeling: Calming, healthy, sustainable
```

**Best for:** Wellness, eco-products, health apps, gardens
**Avoid if:** You need to convey tech sophistication

**Example prompt:**
> "Design a wellness tracker in Biophilic style - soft green gradients, leaf-shaped progress indicators, warm wood-tone accents, organic curves"

---

### Maximalist
**More is more**

```
Colors: Many! Bold combinations, patterns on patterns
Typography: Mixed fonts, decorative, expressive
Shapes: Layered, overlapping, complex
Feeling: Energetic, creative, joyful, overwhelming (intentionally)
```

**Best for:** Creative agencies, entertainment, fashion, art
**Avoid if:** You need clarity or quick scanning

**Example prompt:**
> "Design a hero section in Maximalist style - layered colorful shapes, mixed typography sizes, pattern backgrounds, bold gradients, multiple visual focal points"

---

### Zen / Japanese
**Intentional simplicity, balanced harmony**

```
Colors: Off-white, black, single accent (often red), natural wood
Typography: Clean, plenty of space, asymmetric balance
Shapes: Minimal, intentional negative space, subtle
Feeling: Peaceful, focused, thoughtful
```

**Best for:** Meditation apps, tea brands, galleries, writing tools
**Avoid if:** You need to grab attention quickly

**Example prompt:**
> "Design a reading interface in Zen style - generous margins, black text on cream, single red accent for bookmarks, asymmetric but balanced layout"

---

### Retro-Futurism
**Yesterday's vision of tomorrow**

```
Colors: Orange, teal, chrome, cream, atomic age palette
Typography: Space-age fonts, optimistic curves
Shapes: Rounded rectangles, rays, starbursts, orbital rings
Feeling: Optimistic, nostalgic, playful, sci-fi
```

**Best for:** Games, entertainment, creative tools, music
**Avoid if:** You need to seem cutting-edge or serious

**Example prompt:**
> "Design a music player in Retro-Futurism style - rounded chrome controls, starburst decorations, orange and teal color scheme, space-age typography"

---

### Memphis
**1980s Italian design rebellion**

```
Colors: Bright pink, yellow, teal, black, squiggle patterns
Typography: Geometric, bold, playful
Shapes: Squiggles, terrazzo patterns, mixed geometries
Feeling: Fun, irreverent, energetic, youthful
```

**Best for:** Youth brands, creative tools, games, events
**Avoid if:** You need to seem mature or professional

**Example prompt:**
> "Design a signup form in Memphis style - bright geometric shapes, terrazzo pattern background, bold sans-serif, playful squiggle decorations"

---

### Corporate Modern
**Clean professionalism**

```
Colors: Blue, white, gray, one brand accent
Typography: Professional sans-serif (Inter, SF Pro)
Shapes: Rounded corners, subtle shadows, clear hierarchy
Feeling: Trustworthy, efficient, modern
```

**Best for:** B2B software, enterprise, professional services
**Avoid if:** You need to stand out or seem creative

**Example prompt:**
> "Design a data table in Corporate Modern style - clean white background, subtle gray borders, blue links, clear typography hierarchy, hover states"

---

### Swiss / International
**Grid-based precision**

```
Colors: Often black and white with one bright accent
Typography: Helvetica or similar, strict grid alignment
Shapes: Rectangular, aligned to grid, asymmetric balance
Feeling: Precise, reliable, timeless
```

**Best for:** Information design, publications, professional tools
**Avoid if:** You need warmth or personality

**Example prompt:**
> "Design a documentation page in Swiss style - strict grid layout, Helvetica typography, red accent for links, generous margins, clear information hierarchy"

---

### Organic
**Soft, human, imperfect**

```
Colors: Warm neutrals, pastels, earth tones
Typography: Slightly rounded, friendly
Shapes: Blob shapes, hand-drawn feeling, imperfect curves
Feeling: Approachable, human, comfortable
```

**Best for:** Startups, personal brands, creative agencies
**Avoid if:** You need to convey precision or authority

**Example prompt:**
> "Design a testimonial card in Organic style - blob-shaped container, warm pastel background, rounded avatar, hand-drawn style quotation marks"

---

### Glassmorphism
**Frosted glass effect**

```
Colors: Translucent whites, subtle gradients behind glass
Typography: Clean, high contrast for readability
Shapes: Rounded cards with blur and transparency
Feeling: Modern, airy, layered, iOS-inspired
```

**Best for:** Mobile apps, dashboards, modern web apps
**Avoid if:** You need high contrast accessibility

**Example prompt:**
> "Design a notification panel in Glassmorphism style - frosted glass card with background blur, subtle white border, rounded corners, floating over gradient background"

---

### Neubrutalism
**Bold, loud, in-your-face (modern brutalism)**

```
Colors: Bright primaries, black outlines, white backgrounds
Typography: Heavy, often quirky or display fonts
Shapes: Thick black borders, offset shadows, hand-drawn feel
Feeling: Playful, bold, anti-minimalist
```

**Best for:** Creative portfolios, startup landing pages, blogs
**Avoid if:** You need subtlety or corporate trust

**Example prompt:**
> "Design a feature card in Neubrutalism style - thick black border, offset black shadow, bright yellow background, bold display font, hand-drawn icons"

---

### Cottagecore
**Rural romanticism**

```
Colors: Soft sage, dusty rose, cream, brown
Typography: Vintage serifs, script accents
Shapes: Soft, hand-drawn, floral elements
Feeling: Cozy, nostalgic, wholesome, handmade
```

**Best for:** Food blogs, crafts, lifestyle, small businesses
**Avoid if:** You need to seem tech-forward

**Example prompt:**
> "Design a recipe card in Cottagecore style - cream paper texture, vintage serif headings, soft sage accents, hand-drawn floral border elements"

## Mix and Match

You can combine elements! Try these mashups:

| Combo | What You Get |
|-------|-------------|
| Nordic + Biophilic | Calm, nature-focused, Scandinavian sustainability |
| Brutalist + Cyberpunk | Aggressive futurism, hacker aesthetic |
| Art Deco + Corporate | Luxury business, premium SaaS |
| Memphis + Neubrutalism | Maximum playfulness, Gen-Z appeal |
| Minimalist + Zen | Ultimate calm, meditation app vibes |
| Retro-Futurism + Organic | Friendly sci-fi, approachable tech |

**Example combo prompt:**
> "Design a dashboard combining Nordic and Biophilic styles - clean Scandinavian layout with nature-inspired green accents and leaf-shaped icons"

## Using Styles with WordPress

When requesting styled components, add WordPress context:

```
"Design a WordPress block settings panel in Nordic style"
"Create a Gutenberg sidebar in Minimalist aesthetic"
"Style a WooCommerce product card with Art Deco elements"
"Design a block theme template in Brutalist style"
```

The AI will apply the style while maintaining WordPress patterns and accessibility requirements.
