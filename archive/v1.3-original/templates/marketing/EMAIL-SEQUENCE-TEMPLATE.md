## Email Sequence Metrics to Track

### Overall Sequence Performance
- **Sequence completion rate:** % who receive all 5 emails
- **Unsubscribe rate:** % who opt out (benchmark: <2%)
- **Reply rate:** % who respond to your emails
- **Overall engagement:** Open and click rates across sequence

### Email-Specific Metrics
- **Email 1:** Activation rate (did they complete setup?)
- **Email 2:** Feature adoption rate (did they try the feature?)
- **Email 3:** Engagement rate (clicks to case study, replies)
- **Email 4:** Advanced feature usage
- **Email 5:** Community join rate OR premium conversion

### Optimization Testing
Test these elements:
- Subject lines (A/B test each email)
- Send times (morning vs afternoon)
- Email length (shorter vs longer)
- CTA placement (middle vs end)
- Personal tone vs professional

## Customization Notes

**Timing Adjustments:**
- B2B audiences: Avoid weekend sends
- B2C audiences: Weekend sends often perform well
- Adjust spacing based on user engagement

**Personalization Tokens:**
- [FirstName]
- [PluginInstallDate]
- [LastActiveDate]
- [FeaturesUsed]
- [UserType] (free/premium)

**Segmentation Ideas:**
- Active users vs inactive users
- Free vs premium
- Use case or industry
- Feature usage patterns
- Engagement level

---

## Tips for Success

1. **Keep it conversational:** Write like you're emailing a friend
2. **Be generous with help:** Respond to all replies personally
3. **Test everything:** A/B test subject lines, send times, content
4. **Monitor metrics:** Check open rates, clicks, and conversions weekly
5. **Iterate:** Update emails based on common questions and feedback
6. **Respect inbox:** Don't send too frequently; quality over quantity
7. **Make unsubscribe easy:** Respect people's preferences
8. **Mobile optimize:** 50%+ will read on mobile devices
